All are created by our premium selection of ingredient, handled by well-trained staff. D’SAFA Cafe is an impeccable café, serving a large variety of desserts, fresh and scrumptious ‘popis’, and signature cookies destined to bring warmth and happiness to individuals. We only use the most high-class ingredients that bring harmonious glints to dishes created. All are handled and created with care. D'SAFA CAFE name are taken by our name. Eventhough, our background are not from business cources but my team and I also interest on a bussiness until we succesfull to established our business.
